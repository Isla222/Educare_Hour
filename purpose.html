PURPOSE


Our website name, “Educare Hour”, has meaning for each and every word. We CARE about your education , your goal and your dream and we are here to support you. We would like to educate and level up your knowledge on Web Development. Our website is open for every HOUR. You can visit and learn anytime.

This website is made up of what our team has learnt. One of the purposes is to spread what we’ve learnt. Our “Educare Hour”team created this helpful website to help the beginners to know the basic steps to write the HTML and CSS codes.

We want to persuade the young learners to be interested in Coding as if you’re interested in Computer Science, our website page can’t fully help you. But this will help you to get the ideas for HTML and CSS. You only need to visit just this website to know and get general ideas of HTML and CSS. Let’s enjoy learning!
